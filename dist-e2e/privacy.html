Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

Taxi Pro ("the App") is a ride-hailing application for the Pi Network ecosystem. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

Data we collect

  • Pi account basics — your Pi username and app-scoped user ID, provided by the Pi Network SDK when you sign in. We never see your Pi wallet passphrase or private keys.
  • Location — your device location while you use the App, to show nearby drivers, set pickup points, and navigate rides. Location is not collected in the background.
  • Ride data — pickup/destination addresses, route, fare, ride status, ratings, and in-ride chat messages, kept to operate the service and show your ride history.
  • Payment records — Pi payment identifiers and amounts for rides and tips. Payments are processed by the Pi Network platform; we store only the transaction references.
  • Driver documents — if you register as a driver: vehicle details and licence information you submit for verification.

How we use data

  • To match passengers with drivers and operate rides.
  • To process Pi payments for fares and tips.
  • To keep the service safe (ratings, reports, SOS, fraud prevention).
  • To show your ride history and earnings.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing.
  • We do not use third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not collect email addresses, phone contacts, or government IDs beyond what driver verification requires.

Third-party services

Maps and address search use OpenStreetMap-based services (tiles, Nominatim geocoding, OSRM routing). Optional chat translation uses public translation APIs; only the message text being translated is sent. Weather-based pricing uses anonymous coordinates via Open-Meteo.

Data retention & deletion

Ride and payment records are kept while your account is active. To request deletion of your data, contact us and we will remove your account data within 30 days, except records we must keep for safety or legal reasons.
